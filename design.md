## 1. Brand Identity Overview

### What is Brand Identity?

Brand Identity is the overall representation of TIGERSOFT, conveyed through personality, voice, colors, ways of working, and communication across all channels. No matter who the customer meets or what they discuss, they must feel: **"This is TIGERSOFT."**

> "A brand is not just a logo or company colors, but the identity that customers perceive and remember us by, through every experience they encounter."

- Secondary colors: Use no more than **15%**.

## 13. Website UI Guidelines

- Background: **White (#FFFFFF)**
- Accent / CTA / Headline: **Vivid Red (#F4001A)**
- Dark sections: **Oxford Blue (#0B1F38)**
- Support/UI elements: **UFO Green (#50C8B5)** for success states
- Glassmorphism effects can be used on dark backgrounds
- Use gradient (Red → Oxford Blue) for the hero section
- Rounded corners (border-radius: 12–16px)
- Grid-based layout
- Margin/Padding: 40px all around for sections
